About the Book

Your digital life deserves real protection

Every day, millions of people get hacked, scammed, or have their personal data stolen — and most of them never saw it coming. The scary part? It's not because they were careless. It's because nobody ever showed them what to look for.

Secure Living in a Digital Age changes that. This book walks you through exactly what threats exist online, how to recognize them, and the simple, proven steps you can take today to protect yourself — no technical background needed.
